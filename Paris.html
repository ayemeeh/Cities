✈ Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. Its nickname is the City of Light and it is the largest city in France with a population of 12 million people.

As a traffic center, Paris has a great underground subway system called the Metro. It has any famous art museums and historical buildings like the Eiffel Tower, Palace of Versailles, and the Louvre.
